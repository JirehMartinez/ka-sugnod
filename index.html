A timeless Filipino classic simmered in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, peppercorns, and bay leaves.

Sinigang na Baboy

A refreshing sour-tamarind pork soup that blends tender meat with fresh vegetables.

Loved for its tangy broth and comforting warmth, Sinigang is a staple Filipino dish perfect

Tortang Talong

A well-loved grilled eggplant omelet with a smoky aroma, tender texture, and satisfying flavor.

Often enjoyed with rice and condiments, it remains one of the most versatile Filipino egg dishes.
